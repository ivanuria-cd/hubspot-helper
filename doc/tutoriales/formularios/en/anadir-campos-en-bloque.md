# Bulk-add the missing fields

**Prerequisites:** a form linked to an origin with incomplete coverage (see "Review a form's coverage").
**Estimated time:** 3 minutes

When a form is missing fields that its origin requires, the app can prepare them all at once as a pending change, ready for you to review and apply.

## Steps

1. Go to **CRM → Forms** and open the incomplete form.
2. In the coverage report, find the origin with missing fields.
3. Click **Add missing fields (N)**.
4. An "add fields" **pending change** is created that includes only the missing fields (existing ones are kept).
5. Review and apply the change from **Pending changes** (see "Sync changes with HubSpot").

## Expected result

A pending change appears with the summary "Add N field(s)…". Nothing is written to HubSpot until you apply it.

## FAQ

**Are fields duplicated?** No. Only the missing ones are added; fields already present in the form are left untouched.

**What options are the fields added with?** As visible, non-required fields by default. Fine-tuning of styling and required status is handled afterwards in HubSpot.
