# CRM overview

**Prerequisites:** an open project.
**Estimated time:** 1 minute

The **CRM** screen summarizes the three CRM work areas —Properties, Custom objects and Forms— at a glance.

## Steps

1. In the side menu, click **CRM**.
2. Each card shows the **total** number of items in the area and how many **pending changes** you have (or "Up to date" if there are none).
3. Click **Open** on a card to go to that area and work on it.
4. If HubSpot is not connected, you'll see a notice with a link to configure it (without a portal there is no CRM data).

## Expected result

You know at a glance how much you have in each CRM area and where there is pending work.

## FAQ

**How is it different from the Dashboard?** The Dashboard covers the whole project (connectors and pending items). This view focuses only on the CRM and adds the total number of items per area.
